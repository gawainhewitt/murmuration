Murmuration
Murmuration.London allows people to explore online music making and share this with others to create their own collaborative pieces of music. The website regularly updates so that people can explore different online musical elements.

This work was commissioned by the University of Bristol and UKRI ‘Sensing Spaces of Healthcare’ project (2020-2027), which rethought NHS hospitals through a focus on sensory experiences. The project was delivered in partnership with GOSH Arts.
